iModule

iModule is a self-paced interactive course module for website and mobile application.

The 'i' in iModule represents its emphasis on interactivity, fostering engaging and participatory learning experiences.

Learners can progress through the courses at their own pace, making it a flexible and convenient platform for acquiring knowledge and skills. With its user-friendly interface and accessibility on various devices.

iModule empowers learners to explore a diverse range of lessons while enjoying an enriching educational journey.
